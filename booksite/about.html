About The Book

This is a book that is geared towards helping an individual unlock the limitless part of his or her life regardless of the sphere of influence they are operating from. Going through the book will expose you to a new kind of paradigm shift to help you access the true capacity that is embedded in you. The book is a seven days material which in it has daily action plans and affirmations to enable you meditate and reflect on the topic you just concluded. We made the book to be so easy to read and relatable, such that we used lot of illustrations to give you clarity.
